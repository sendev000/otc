Skipping the symptoms analyzer will take you straight to an over-the-counter recommendation?

  • You know what your symptoms are and just like to know what to take over-the-counter.

  • You are reviewing the recommendation one more time.

  • You are leaving a comment or contributing to the pharmacist's recommendation.

  • You are contacting the pharmacist.
