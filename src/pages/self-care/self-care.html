Who is this for?
A pharmacist will recommend an over-the-counter (OTC) medication for your symptom using interactive articles.

First we will rule out any emergency situation.

Then you will choose from a list of common symptoms you will need advice on.

Next we will determine if it is something you can take care of with over-the-counter (OTC) means or if you must see a healthcare provider for evaluation.

If you can take care of it with OTC measures, a pharmacist's interactive article will guide you towards getting the best over-the-counter relief.

Else, the article will advice you to talk to a healthcare provider and tell you why.
