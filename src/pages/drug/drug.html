FYI:

  • Manufacturer's coupons has limits on how much they can take off. For this reason, $4 copay does not mean $4 copay. You are responsible for anything above the maximum payable amount.
  • All do not apply to Government programs such as medicaid, medicare, Federal employee, Tricare and so on.
  • Some programs require:
    - You to fill out forms
    - You to print the coupon
    - Downloading the coupon as a PDF
    - Sending the coupon to your email.
