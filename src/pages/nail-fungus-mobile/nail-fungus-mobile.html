Is this a reoccurrence or first time nail fungal infection?

Reoccurrence First time

 

Seek urgent care or call the doctor.

Here is why:

  • Nail fungus are hard to treat.

  • You will need prescription oral antifungal medications and stay with it all the way.

  • About 50% of people with nail fungal infection get successful treatment first time around with preescription antifungal.

  • In some cases, the health care provider may remove the nail. Nails grow slowly.

  • Even if treatment is successful, a new, clearer nail may take up to a year to grow in.
