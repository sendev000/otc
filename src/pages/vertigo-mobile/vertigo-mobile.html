Are you diabetic?

 

Yes?
Very low blood sugar can cause dizziness which can be confused with vertigo.

Just starting a new diabetic drug or increasing the dose of the current one you are taking can lower the blood sugar level suprisingly making you feel dizzy.

If you are also sweating, we would suspect this and advice you to discuss this with your doctor.

We suggest you check your blood sugar level and make sure it is at recommended levels.
