If fruity or sweet, it can be a sign of ketoacidosis.
This may occur in diabetics.

If you are diabetic, it means the body is trying to get rid of excess acetone through the breath.

This is life-threatening.
There is a lack of insulin. Which means the body cannot use your glucose as a source of fuel. So it is now using fat instead.

Ketone is the byproducts of fat breakdown and they are building up in your blood.

If you have missed a dose of insulin, go ahead and use it now. You need insulin. If not, then go to the nearest emergency room. This can be life threatening.
