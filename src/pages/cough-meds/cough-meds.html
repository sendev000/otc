Cough Medications

 


Answer a few questions to compare OTC cough medications more efficiently.

This will assume you are comparing cough medications to see which would work best for you.

 

Are you experiencing a dry cough?

That is, you are not coughing  up stuff?

 

Yes?
Get a cough syrup with dextromethorphan as an active ingredient.

 

Is the cough wet and producing mucus?

A  feeling of heaviness on your chest?

 

Yes?
You will need something to make the mucus thinner so you can spit it out.

A cough medicine with guaifenesin as an active ingredient would suffice.

This is an expectorant  that will thin the mucus making it easier for you to cough it up and clear the breathing passages.

Be sure to drink a lot of water with this. 

 

Is this a dry cough that keeps you up at night?

 

Yes?
A cough syrup with dextromethorphan as an active ingredient will be good.

 

Is this a wet cough that is producing mucus and keeping you up at night?

 

Yes?
You will need a cough medication with Guaifenesin + dextrometorphan.

 

Like to know if you should get the cough syrup or tablets?

 

Yes?
This will depend on the factors listed below.

List reasons to get the syrup here.

 

Long acting (12hr), maximum strength or regular strength. Like to know what you should get?

 

Yes?
If the cough keeps you up at night, get the 12 hour cough syrup or tablets.

With the regular acting (every 4 to 6 hours) cough syrups or tablets, you don't want the cough waking you up around 4 or 5 in the morning when the effects of the medication wears off.

Nothing as bad as starting your day with a morning cough. You will have a headache the rest of the day.

If you have a sore throat stay away from the maximum strength 12 hr tablets.    These will be too big for you to swallow. The pills are  big. Should you need a 12 hour cough relief tablet and you have a sore throat, stick with the regular strength. One tablet should do it for most people. But if you need the maximum strength dose, just take it twice.

 

Cough drops. Like to know if you should get these too?

 

Yes?
Get these if your throat is also sore. Maybe from the coughing.

Cough drops will help soothe the throat and suppress the cough.    

Also get cough drops if you are experiencing a tickly cough.

 

Assuming you need a cough drop. Like to know what type to get?

So many different types out there. We can help you with that.

 

Yes?
You should get the cough drops with menthol as an active ingredient.

The higher the strength the better? May be. fisherman's friend contains 10 mg of menthol, Halls intense cool (15 mg menthol), ricola extra strength (8.3 mg menthol).

Bear in mind too that some are uniquely formulated blends. Like ricola.

Here is the deal, stay with the higher strength if you also have nasal congestion with this cough and soreness of the throat.

 


Alright. We just went through choosing the cough medication that will work best for you.

Remember to seek urgent care if:

  • this cough is so bad that you get a headache when you cough
  • you can't sleep at night because of the cough for more than 3 nights now.
  • coughing up blood.
