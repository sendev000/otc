Was this sharp pain followed by bloody drainage from the ear canal?

 

Seek urgent care or emergency room.

  • You may be looking at a ruptured eardrum.

  • Quick treatment is important to preserve your hearing.

  • Complications such as hearing loss can develop.

  • Risking middle ear infection (otitis media).
    Bacteria can enter your middle ear which might lead to infection that can cause hearing loss later.

  • Complications due to middle ear cyst (cholesteatoma).
    This cyst contains ear wax, dusts, bacteria which the ear drum usually keep out. If your eardrum is ruptured, the debris can pass into your middle ear and form a cyst providing a friendly place for bacteria to grow.
